About YFCMUNCLAVE

Youth for Change Model United Nations is an event which aims to educate the youth by creating a proper diplomatic experience which will help flourish their diplomatic skills and encourage their interest in international relations and political arena.

The 3Cs we propose

COMPREHEND

Be a part of committee stimulation and discuss as well as deliberate over an agenda.

CONGENGRATE

Debate possible solutions of each simulation.

CREATE

Execute the solution and help make a change


We believe the debate and discussion being held should be solution oriented and hence our principle, 'From Deliberation to Action'. We wholeheartedly hope and expect that the conference shapes the perspective of the participants in a manner that they strive for the betterment of the world. So their actions help to further instill peace throughout the globe much as the goal of the United Nations.
